Flutter FAB

The FloatingActionButton widget (also known as FAB) floats above the content on the screen and is tied to an action. The FAB widget is circular in shape and resides in the bottom right corner of the screen. You can use the FAB widget to perform the primary action on the screen, such as Add, Create, etc.
